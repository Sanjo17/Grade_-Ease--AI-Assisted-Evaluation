Welcome to,
Grade Ease

Grade-ease, a dynamic web application developed with Django, redefines the evaluation process for online tests by seamlessly integrating advanced pre-trained models like BERT for semantic similarity assessment. It offers educators a comprehensive platform where they can create, administer, and, if necessary, manually edit marks for assessments.

About

Automatic Grading
GradeEase offers a revolutionary solution for automatic grading, capable of evaluating both online subjective answers and responses provided in digital answer sheets. Whether students submit their answers digitally through an online platform or by uploading a scanned copy of their paper sheet, GradeEase employs cutting-edge technology to analyze and assess their responses with precision. Utilizing advanced algorithms , GradeEase compares student answers to model responses or predefined criteria set by the instructor. By seamlessly integrating with various digital platforms and accommodating diverse assessment formats, GradeEase revolutionizes the grading experience for educators and students alike, facilitating efficient and effective evaluation in today's digital learning environment.
Online Test
Grade Ease facilitates the seamless administration and evaluation of online tests across various courses. Educators can effortlessly set examinations for their students, featuring subjective questions that require written responses. When students undertake these exams, Grade Ease employs sophisticated algorithms to compare their answers with the model answers provided by the teacher. Through this evaluation process, the website accurately grades each paper, considering factors such as accuracy, relevance, and coherence. Subsequently, it promptly delivers the results to the students.
Answer Paper Grading
Grade Ease offers a streamlined solution for automatic grading of answer sheets across various courses. With this platform, students can conveniently upload their answer sheets, tailored to their respective courses. Utilizing advanced algorithms , Grade Ease swiftly analyzes the content of the answer sheets, evaluating responses against predefined criteria or model answers. Upon completion of the analysis, the website promptly generates accurate grades and provides detailed feedback to the students.
